REVIVERE - gallery of the ancients
In the distant future, a time when we breathe technology and we’ve wandered across stars, let’s remind ourselves of our home. REVIVERE is a preservation project containing the works of 34 human artists of the past. This small virtual space is our home, it is where our souls have lived again, where we take a stance that art should be authentic, man-made, and alive. We are humans, science and art are the crafts of our very existence.
